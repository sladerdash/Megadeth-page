Meet them up

This band, made up of Dave Mustaine (vocalist and guitarist), Dirk Verbeuren (drummer), James LoMenzo (bassist), and Teemu Mäntysaari (second guitarist), was formed in 1983 after Dave got expelled from the band Metallica, resulting in dave wanting to "revenge" and at the same time show his talent as a thrash metal musician.

Debuting with "Killing Is My Business... And Business Is Good!", they certainly became known, but were the albums "Rust in Peace," "Peace Sell... But Who's Buying?" and "Countdown to Extinction" that would see this band receive recognition.
